# XP Scoring System

Experience points (XP) are the currency of the Level Up AI programme. They measure depth of AI engagement — not just activity — and determine rank progression, leaderboard position, and reward eligibility.

---

## The Four-Stage Model

Every AI adoption behaviour maps to one of four stages. The stages are progressive: later stages require evidence of genuine integration, not just participation.

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  Stage I    · AWARE       · 10 XP   · Know it exists               │
│  Stage II   · TRY         · 25 XP   · First-time use               │
│  Stage III  · INTEGRATE   · 75 XP   · Embedded in workflow         │
│  Stage IV   · SCALE       · 300 XP  · Team-wide, sustained impact  │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

### Stage I — Aware (10 XP)
The member has identified an AI tool, technique, or capability relevant to their role. They have not yet used it, but they have logged it in the team registry and can explain what it does and why it is relevant.

**What counts:** Tool registry entries, attendance at awareness sessions, AI news summaries shared with the team.

---

### Stage II — Try (25 XP)
The member has used an AI tool or technique for the first time in a real work context — not a sandbox, tutorial, or demo environment. The output was used (or seriously considered for use) in actual work.

**What counts:** First use of a tool on a real task, first prompt submitted to an AI system in a work context, first AI-assisted draft submitted for review.

---

### Stage III — Integrate (75 XP)
The member has embedded an AI tool or technique into an existing, recurring workflow. Use is no longer experimental — it is part of how the work is done. A documented outcome (time saved, quality improved, error reduced) is required.

**What counts:** Workflow redesign with AI embedded, 14+ consecutive days of AI-assisted task completion, documented before/after comparison with measurable outcome.

---

### Stage IV — Scale (300 XP)
The member has driven AI adoption beyond their own work, creating measurable, sustained impact at the team or squad level. Evidence must show that others are now working differently as a direct result of this member's efforts.

**What counts:** Team-wide workflow redesign adopted by 3+ colleagues, prompt template used by 3+ team members for 30+ days, AI programme or process formally adopted by the team.

## XP Caps and Limits

To prevent gaming and ensure fair progression, the following caps apply:

| Rule | Limit |
|---|---|
| Maximum XP per week per member | 150 XP |
| Maximum Quest 04 (Evangelism) XP per month | 60 XP |
| Maximum Quest 05 (Learning) XP per month | 50 XP |
| Minimum evidence age before XP is claimed | Same week |
| Maximum retrospective XP claims | 2 weeks prior |

---

## Squad vs. Individual XP

The programme tracks both individual and squad XP. They are calculated separately.

- **Individual XP** — Earned by a single member. Determines personal rank and leaderboard position.
- **Squad XP** — The sum of all individual XP earned by squad members in the current season. Determines the squad leaderboard and seasonal prize eligibility.

Where two members collaborate on a quest activity:
- The member who led the activity earns **100% of the XP**
- The member who supported earns **60% of the XP**
- Both must submit evidence independently

## Penalty XP

Penalties are applied via the Responsible AI Use quest (Quest 06). They are deducted from both individual and squad totals.

| Breach | Individual Deduction | Squad Deduction |
|---|---|---|
| Unsanctioned tool use | −15 XP | −15 XP |
| Data privacy breach | −30 XP | −30 XP |
| Undisclosed AI use in client output | −15 XP | −15 XP |
| Repeated breach (same category) | −30 XP | −30 XP |

Penalties cannot reduce a member's XP below zero. The floor is 0 XP.

---

## XP Validation Process

All XP claims require evidence. The validation flow is:

```
Member submits evidence → Squad Lead reviews → AI Adoption Manager approves → XP recorded
```

Typical turnaround: **3 working days** from submission to approval.

Disputed XP claims may be escalated to the monthly jury review panel, whose decision is final.
