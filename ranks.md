# Mastery Ranks

The Level Up AI programme defines six mastery ranks. Ranks are earned by accumulating individual XP and are permanent within a season — they cannot be lost due to inactivity (only penalty breaches can affect total XP).

Each rank unlocks a specific set of privileges, recognition, and rewards.

---

## Rank Overview

```
🌱  Curious      ·   100 XP  ·  First steps taken
🔍  Explorer     ·   300 XP  ·  Actively discovering AI
🔧  Integrator   ·   600 XP  ·  AI embedded in daily work
🚀  Innovator    · 1,000 XP  ·  Driving AI transformation
🧠  Champion     · 1,400 XP  ·  An AI leader across the org
👑  Sovereign    · 2,000 XP  ·  The highest rank
```

---

## Rank 01 · Curious
**Threshold: 100 XP**

### What It Means
You have completed programme onboarding, activated your first AI tool in a real work context, and logged your first prompt or AI interaction. The journey has begun.

### Unlocks
- Public mention in the weekly team brief
- Curious badge on your internal profile
- Access to the programme's beginner resource library
- Entry on the squad leaderboard

### How to Reach It Quickly
Complete onboarding (10 XP), log two AI tools in the registry (20 XP), use one tool for the first time in a real task (25 XP), and attend a mandatory programme session (20 XP) — that is 75 XP before your first week is out.

---

## Rank 02 · Explorer
**Threshold: 300 XP**

### What It Means
You are actively exploring what AI can do across multiple tools and contexts. You have moved beyond onboarding into genuine, repeated experimentation.

### Unlocks
- Feature on the squad and individual leaderboard with Explorer badge
- Digital Explorer badge shared in the team communications channel
- Access to the intermediate resource library
- Invitation to the monthly Explorer & above peer group session

### How to Reach It Quickly
From Curious (100 XP), earn 200 more: complete a 14-day usage streak (50 XP), map a workflow for AI integration (25 XP), contribute a prompt to the team library (25 XP), and complete an AI literacy course (30 XP). That covers 130 XP — combine with any evangelism or learning activities.

---

## Rank 03 · Integrator
**Threshold: 600 XP**

### What It Means
AI is no longer experimental for you — it is part of how you work. You have permanently redesigned at least one recurring workflow with AI embedded, and the change has been sustained and documented.

### Unlocks
- Spotlight feature at the next monthly all-hands session
- Integrator badge and profile update
- Physical reward package (team gift or equivalent)
- Invitation to the Integrator & above quarterly strategy session with leadership
- Right to formally mentor a Curious or Explorer-rank colleague

### How to Reach It Quickly
From Explorer (300 XP), earn 300 more: complete a full workflow integration with documented time savings (250 XP across all sub-activities), and deliver one internal demo (30 XP). That is 280 XP — supplement with any learning certification.

---

## Rank 04 · Innovator
**Threshold: 1,000 XP**

### What It Means
You are driving AI transformation — not just within your own work, but across your team. Multiple workflows have been redesigned, team demonstrations have been delivered, and measurable time savings have been documented and shared.

### Unlocks
- Formal **AI Champion** title, added to your internal profile and email signature
- Priority access to new AI tools during beta and pilot phases
- Innovator reward package (premium tier)
- Named feature in the monthly internal newsletter
- Invitation to join the AI Adoption Manager programme as an associate

### How to Reach It Quickly
From Integrator (600 XP), earn 400 more: a second workflow integration (up to 300 XP), a published internal case study (40 XP), and two additional peer mentoring sessions (50 XP). That reaches the threshold with room for other activities.

---

## Rank 05 · Champion
**Threshold: 1,400 XP**

### What It Means
You are an AI leader across the organisation. You have earned certifications, published case studies that others are using, and formally mentored multiple colleagues through the programme. Your impact is documented and visible.

### Unlocks
- **AI Champion** status elevated to **Senior AI Champion** — visible to leadership
- Executive visibility: named in leadership communications and business reviews
- Opportunity to represent the organisation in a client-facing AI showcase
- Premium reward tier (highest individual reward package in the programme)
- Invitation to contribute to next season's quest and framework design

### How to Reach It Quickly
From Innovator (1,000 XP), earn 400 more: a business-relevant certification (60 XP), two additional case studies (80 XP), a team prompting session (50 XP), and sustained tool exploration with streak bonuses (150 XP). That is 340 XP — close the gap with evangelism activities.

---

## Rank 06 · Sovereign
**Threshold: 2,000 XP**

### What It Means
The highest rank in the programme. You have fundamentally transformed how your team operates with AI — with documented, sustained, organisation-wide impact. You are not just an early adopter. You are proof of what AI adoption looks like when it is done properly.

### Unlocks
- **Permanent entry in the AI Hall of Fame** — inscribed at season close and maintained indefinitely
- Named and featured in all company-wide internal communications for the season
- Shared with key clients as a named AI leader (with consent)
- The highest-tier reward package in the programme
- Invited to co-design the next season's programme with the AI Adoption leadership team
- Keynote slot at the season-close recognition event

### Notes
Sovereign rank is rare by design. It is expected that fewer than 5% of programme participants will reach this level in any given season. Its scarcity is what makes it meaningful.

---

## Rank Maintenance

Ranks earned in a season are **permanent for that season** and cannot be lost due to inactivity.

Between seasons, all members reset to 0 XP. Returning members who reached a rank in a prior season receive:
- A **returning member badge** acknowledging their prior rank
- A **25 XP head start** for each prior season in which they reached Integrator or above

This ensures that experienced participants are recognised without making the leaderboard uncompetitive for new joiners.
