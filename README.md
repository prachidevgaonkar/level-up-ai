# 🎮 Level Up AI
### A Gamification Framework for AI Adoption

> *"Stop mandating AI. Start gamifying it. Turn compliance into a game worth winning."*

---

## What Is This?

Most AI adoption programmes fail — not because the technology isn't good enough, but because nobody made it feel worth doing.

**Level Up AI** is an open framework that applies gamification principles to AI adoption. Instead of training decks and top-down mandates, it gives your team XP, ranks, leaderboards, and rewards for genuinely engaging with AI — and measures the outcomes that matter.

## The Six Adoption Quests

Each quest targets a specific AI adoption behaviour. XP scales with depth of engagement — awareness earns a foothold, transformation earns authority.

| # | Quest | Max XP |
|---|---|---|
| 01 | 🤖 AI Tool Exploration | 300 XP |
| 02 | ⚡ Workflow Integration | 300 XP |
| 03 | 🧠 Prompt Engineering | 200 XP |
| 04 | 📢 AI Evangelism | 150 XP |
| 05 | 📚 Continuous Learning | 150 XP |
| 06 | 🛡️ Responsible AI Use | −15 XP per breach |

---

## The XP Scoring Model

Every AI adoption behaviour maps to one of four stages. The gap between Stage I and Stage IV is where champions are made.

```
Stage I   · Aware      →  10 XP   Know it exists
Stage II  · Try        →  25 XP   First-time use in a real work context
Stage III · Integrate  →  75 XP   Embedded into an existing workflow
Stage IV  · Scale      → 300 XP   Team-wide impact, documented and sustained
```

## The Four Adoption Metrics

The programme tracks outcomes, not just activity.

- **Tool Activation** — % of team members using at least one AI tool weekly
- **Time Saved** — Average hours saved per person per week through AI
- **Workflows Rebuilt** — Number of processes permanently redesigned with AI
- **AI Champions** — Number of members at Innovator rank or above

---

## The Eight Governance Rules

1. **Squad-based competition** — Teams of 6–10 compete to accumulate the most XP across all six quests.
2. **Weekly evidence submissions** — Squad leads log validated evidence every Friday in the central tracker.
3. **AI Adoption Managers** — Each squad has a dedicated manager who validates claims and coaches stragglers.
4. **Monthly jury review** — A cross-functional panel reviews all progress monthly before XP is finalised.
5. **Live leaderboard, published weekly** — Rankings go out every Monday across the internal AI Adoption Hub.
6. **Monthly reward ceremonies** — Top earners are recognised and rewarded at monthly all-hands sessions.
7. **Wildcard challenges** — Unannounced bonus quests and hackathons keep energy high mid-season.
8. **AI Hall of Fame** — At season close, top performers are permanently recognised across the organisation.

## How to Use This Framework

1. **Define your squads** — Group employees into teams of 6–10 across functions.
2. **Assign an AI Adoption Manager** to each squad for validation and coaching.
3. **Launch with a kickoff session** — Explain the quests, XP model, and ranks.
4. **Set up the tracker** — Use the squad tracker template for weekly submissions.
5. **Publish the leaderboard** — Share rankings every Monday without fail.
6. **Run your first reward ceremony** — Don't wait until the end of the season.
7. **Introduce a wildcard** — Surprise the org with a bonus challenge in Week 6.
8. **Close the season** — Induct your top performers into the AI Hall of Fame.
