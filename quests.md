# The Six Adoption Quests

Each quest targets a specific AI adoption behaviour. Experience points scale from awareness through to measurable, sustained organisational impact. Teams earn XP at each stage — identification, proposal, qualification, and implementation.

---

## Quest 01 · AI Tool Exploration
**Max XP: 300 · Colour: Indigo**

> *Discover and activate AI tools relevant to your role.*

### Description
Earn XP by discovering, trialling, and onboarding AI tools. Points scale from logging a new tool relevant to your role all the way through to sustained daily use over thirty or more consecutive days. This quest rewards breadth of exploration and depth of commitment equally.

### XP Breakdown
| Activity | XP |
|---|---|
| Log a new AI tool in the team registry | 10 |
| Use an AI tool for the first time in a real work context | 25 |
| Maintain active weekly use for 14 consecutive days | 50 |
| Maintain active weekly use for 30+ consecutive days | 150 |
| Document a specific, measurable outcome from tool use | 65 |

### Evidence Required
- Screenshot or log entry confirming tool activation
- Usage log (dates + context) for streak milestones
- Written outcome note for documented impact claims

### Notes
- Only tools on the organisation's approved AI tools list are eligible
- Personal productivity tools count if used in a direct work context
- Streaks reset if a full calendar week passes without logged use

## Quest 02 · Workflow Integration
**Max XP: 300 · Colour: Cyan**

> *Permanently embed AI into existing ways of working.*

### Description
The true measure of adoption is behaviour change, not tool installation. This quest rewards those who go beyond trying AI in isolation and instead redesign how real work gets done. Points scale from mapping an existing workflow through to permanently replacing a prior method with an AI-assisted version and documenting the impact.

### XP Breakdown
| Activity | XP |
|---|---|
| Map an existing workflow and identify an AI insertion point | 25 |
| Run an AI-assisted pilot on the identified workflow | 50 |
| Document time or quality improvement from the pilot | 75 |
| Permanently adopt the AI-assisted version (30+ days) | 100 |
| Share the redesigned workflow with the wider team | 50 |

### Evidence Required
- Before/after workflow diagram or written description
- Pilot log with dates and context
- Documented outcome: time saved, error rate, quality measure
- Confirmation from squad lead that change is permanent

### Notes
- The workflow must be a recurring, real work process — not a one-off task
- Time savings must be estimated conservatively and validated by the squad lead
- Workflows redesigned by a pair count for both participants at 80% XP each

## Quest 03 · Prompt Engineering
**Max XP: 200 · Colour: Green**

> *Develop the craft of communicating with AI systems.*

### Description
The quality of AI output is determined entirely by the quality of the input. This quest rewards those who invest in learning how to communicate with AI systems effectively — and who share what they learn with the team. Points are earned for structured learning, prompt contribution, and demonstrating measurable improvement in AI output quality.

### XP Breakdown
| Activity | XP |
|---|---|
| Complete a structured prompt engineering course or module | 40 |
| Contribute a high-performing prompt to the team library | 25 |
| Publish a before/after comparison showing prompt impact | 35 |
| Build a prompt template adopted by 3+ colleagues | 50 |
| Host a team session on prompting techniques | 50 |

### Evidence Required
- Course completion certificate or confirmation
- Prompt library submission with context and use case
- Before/after output screenshots with commentary
- Adoption confirmation from colleagues (DM or comment)

### Notes
- Prompts submitted to the library are reviewed by the AI Adoption Manager before XP is awarded
- "High-performing" is defined as producing measurably better output than a baseline prompt
- This quest has no breach penalty but submitted prompts that are rejected do not earn XP

## Quest 04 · AI Evangelism
**Max XP: 150 · Colour: Amber**

> *Accelerate adoption by spreading AI capability through the team.*

### Description
A single visible champion accelerates adoption more effectively than ten hours of mandatory training. This quest rewards those who invest their own time and expertise in bringing colleagues along — through demonstrations, mentoring, published case studies, and team showcases that others can learn from and replicate.

### XP Breakdown
| Activity | XP |
|---|---|
| Run an internal AI demo for 3+ colleagues | 30 |
| Deliver a 1:1 mentoring session with a less-experienced colleague | 25 |
| Present a documented AI win at a team meeting | 30 |
| Publish an internal case study in the programme repository | 40 |
| Onboard a colleague to a new AI tool end-to-end | 25 |

### Evidence Required
- Attendee list or confirmation for demos and meetings
- Mentee confirmation for 1:1 sessions
- Case study document submitted using the standard template
- Colleague confirmation for tool onboarding

### Notes
- Self-promotion of personal projects does not qualify — the content must be useful and replicable
- Case studies must use the provided template and be reviewed by the squad lead
- The same demo delivered to the same audience twice earns XP only once

## Quest 05 · Continuous Learning
**Max XP: 150 · Colour: Rose**

> *Certify, upskill, and remain current as AI evolves.*

### Description
The AI landscape evolves at a pace that makes any static training programme obsolete within months. This quest rewards those who take ownership of their own learning — pursuing certifications, attending webinars, completing structured courses, and formally sharing key learnings with colleagues in a way that turns individual development into collective capability.

### XP Breakdown
| Activity | XP |
|---|---|
| Complete a business-relevant AI certification | 60 |
| Complete an AI literacy course (vendor-agnostic) | 30 |
| Attend a relevant external AI webinar or conference session | 20 |
| Share a structured learning summary with the team | 20 |
| Complete 100% attendance at all mandatory programme sessions | 20 |

### Evidence Required
- Certification or course completion certificate
- Webinar attendance confirmation or notes
- Learning summary shared in the team channel with 3+ key takeaways

### Notes
- Certifications must be directly relevant to the team's work to qualify for full XP
- Certifications with no relevance to current or planned team activities earn 20 XP (partial)
- Mandatory programme sessions must be attended in full — partial attendance earns no XP

## Quest 06 · Responsible AI Use
**Penalty: −15 XP per breach · Colour: Slate**

> *Ethics, data privacy, safety, and compliance.*

### Description
Capability without responsibility is a liability. This is the only quest governed entirely by penalties rather than rewards. It exists to protect the organisation, its clients, and its people from the risks that come with poorly governed AI use. All members are expected to adhere to the programme's responsible use standards at all times — and breaches result in XP deductions.

### Breach Categories
| Breach Type | XP Deduction |
|---|---|
| Use of an unsanctioned AI tool on work data | −15 XP |
| Sharing confidential or client data with an AI system | −30 XP |
| Using AI-generated output in client work without review | −15 XP |
| Failure to disclose AI use where disclosure is required | −15 XP |
| Repeated breach (same category, same member, same season) | −30 XP |

### Evidence & Process
- Breaches are reported by squad leads or AI Adoption Managers
- Members have the right to contest a breach within 5 working days
- Contested breaches are reviewed by the monthly jury panel
- Confirmed breaches are logged in the central tracker and deducted from the squad's total XP

### Notes
- This quest has no positive XP — it is a floor, not a ceiling
- The approved tools list is published in the programme hub and updated monthly
- Ignorance of the responsible use policy is not accepted as grounds for appeal after onboarding
